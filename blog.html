Introduction

Hi, I'm Lara Stefani, a senior at The College of New Jersey (TCNJ), and I'm thrilled to be graduating next year. My passion for creativity and design has been a driving force in my academic and personal journey. I'm excited to showcase some of my design work here—ranging from earlier pieces that reflect my foundational skills to recent projects that highlight my growth and adaptability as a designer.

My creative journey started with a deep interest in visual storytelling and design, a passion that has only grown as I've explored different mediums like photography, graphic design, and web design. Each project has been an opportunity to challenge myself, refine my craft, and develop new techniques to solve creative problems effectively.

What excites me the most about design is its ability to communicate ideas, evoke emotions, and bring people together. Whether it's through a thoughtfully designed logo, an engaging social media graphic, or an interactive website layout, I strive to create work that leaves a lasting impression and tells a compelling story.
