My Journey

1. Hi, I'm Lara Stefani, a senior at The College of New Jersey (TCNJ), and I'm thrilled to be graduating next year. I'm excited to showcase some of my design work here—ranging from earlier pieces to projects I've been developing recently. My creative journey started with a passion for visual storytelling and design, which has grown as I explored different mediums like photography, graphic design, and web design. I can't wait to share my work wit h you and look forward to connecting with others who are just as passionate about design! .

Key Features of my Portfolio

My portfolio is more than just a collection of my work. It is a carefully curated experience, highlighting my projects in an accessible and engaging way.
Some key features include:

1. Gallery: Users can click through a gallery of my projects, with each piece linking to a detailed breakdown of the design process.
2. Mobile-Friendly Design: The website is fully responsive and accessible across devices, ensuring a seamless experience whether you’re on a desktop or mobile.
